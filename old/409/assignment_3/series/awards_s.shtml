Firefly not only was nominated for many awards, it also won an Emmy!

Awards Won:
  • Emmy Awards:
    • Outstanding Special Visual Effects for a Series
  • Saturn Awards:
    • Best DVD Television Release (For "The Complete Firefly")
    • Cinescape Grand Face of the Future Award - Male: Nathan Fillion
  • Visiual Effects Society Awards
    • Best Visual Effects in a Television Series
Nominations:
  • Hugo Awards:
    • Best Dramatic Presentation - Short Form
      • For episode "Heart of Gold"
      • For episode "The Message"
      • For episode "Serenity"
  • Motion Picture Sound Editors, USA:
    • Golden Reel Award - Best Sound Editing in Television Long Form - Sound Effects and Foley
  • Golden Satellite Awards
    • Best DVD Extras
  • Visiual Effects Society Awards
    • Best Compositing in a Television Program, Music Video, or Commercial
